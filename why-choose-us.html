Why choose us?

Because Your Business Deserves the Best.

Sure, Wix and Squarespace are popular tools, and they can seem affordable upfront. But there’s a big difference between using those platforms and working with me. Here’s why choosing me is the better option for your business.”

1. Customization & Professionalism:

“With Wix or Squarespace, you’re limited to cookie-cutter templates that can look like hundreds of other websites. I design and build a website tailored specifically to your brand, so it stands out and feels unique. This professional look and feel gives your business credibility and makes a lasting impression on your customers.”

2. Performance & SEO:

“DIY platforms often come with bloated code that can slow your site down and hurt your search engine rankings. I create lean, fast-loading websites optimized for speed and SEO, helping you rank better on Google and provide a smoother experience for your visitors.”

3. Ownership & Control:

“When you use Wix or Squarespace, you’re locked into their ecosystem—you never truly own your website. With me, you’ll have full control over your site and hosting, so you’re not tied to any one platform. If you ever need to move your site or scale it up, you can do so easily.”

4. Support & Long-Term Value:

“DIY platforms leave you on your own. If something breaks or you need to make changes, you’re stuck figuring it out yourself—or paying someone later to fix it. I offer ongoing support and am here to help if you need updates, advice, or troubleshooting. Plus, my hosting and maintenance services mean you don’t need to worry about anything technical.”

5. Cost vs. Time & Effort:
“While Wix may seem cheaper upfront, it takes a lot of time and effort to make a site that looks even halfway decent. Plus, many features—like custom functionality or advanced analytics—are locked behind expensive premium plans. I save you time, handle everything for you, and provide a solution that’s custom-built for your needs. It’s a smarter investment for the long-term success of your business.”

“So while DIY platforms might be okay for hobbyists, businesses like yours deserve a professional website that reflects the quality of your work and helps you grow. That’s what I deliver, and it’s why so many people prefer working with a developer like me.”
